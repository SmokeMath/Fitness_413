ABOUT


At Fitness 4:13, we are more than just a gym. We are a welcoming community where your physical fitness and faith can come together. Established in 2019, Fitness 4:13 is driven by a passion to share the transformative power of faith and fitness with our community. As the owner, I have created a space that supports and inspires individuals like you to achieve your wellness goals.

Our mission is to motivate, support, and hold you accountable on your fitness and wellness journey.

Join us at Fitness 4:13 and embark on a transformative journey. Let us guide you towards a healthier, more purposeful life through fitness and faith.
